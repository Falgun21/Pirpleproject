Ginger Tea Recipe

Ginger tea can help relieve congestion associated with the common cold. It improves blood circulation, relieves stress, strengthens immunity, etc.

    Ingredients required:

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Tea leaves
  • Milk

    Steps to follow:

  1. Put the water in the vessel and boil it for some time.🚰

  2. Add some tea leaves.🍃

  3. Add sugar and ginger.🍬

  4. Wait till it boils.☕

  5. And then add milk.🥛

  6. Your Ginger Tea is ready.☕
